The Story

Uptown Brewing Company's BILLY BEER is a light beer named after founder Billy Dunn. Despite initial reservations due to the stigma surrounding light beers, the craft brewery embraced the style to create a drinkable, crushable beer for gamedays and tailgates. Customers quickly praised it as the best light beer they'd ever had, solidifying its place on the brewery's menu and their mission to offer quality craft beers to North Carolina.
